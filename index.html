About Us

Sun Hing Foods, Inc., established in 1981, is a well-known ethnic food wholesaler and distributor in the United States and Canada. With previous experience in manufacturing dairy products overseas, Sun Hing Foods, Inc. is the market leader in serving high quality dairy and ethnic food products to Asian, West Indian and Hispanic communities.

Sun Hing Foods prides itself on being capable of accommodating and adapting to the ever-changing needs of its customers. Our company continuously strives to provide unsurpassed quality of services and support to the cultural preservation and growth of the communities it serves. Consumers recognize our brands for its high quality standard.

A Brief History

  • 1981: founded in North Beach area of San Francisco, California
  • 1983: launched distribution to the East Coast
  • 1985: opened regional office in Los Angeles, California
  • 1990: launched distribution to Canada
  • 1991: established 6 nationwide distribution centers
  • 1992: moved corporate office to China Basin area within San Francisco
  • 1993: began marketing products to Caribbean customers
  • 1994: began marketing products to Hispanic customers
  • 1997: began selling to bakery customers
  • 1998: moved corporate office to South San Francisco
  • 2013: moved regional office to new location
